## 2. Customer Base

- **Who:** Men, style-conscious, modern. Not flashy — intentional.
- **Mindset:** Want jewelry that means something. Self-expression > status flex.
- **Lifestyle:** Active, casual-cool. Beach, sweat, water — jewelry must survive it.
- **Value driver:** Quality + durability + design they won't see on everyone.
- **Price sensitivity:** Mid-luxury. Pay for craft and longevity, not logos.

---

## 3. Brand Values

1. **Quality craftsmanship** — "חומרי גלם איכותיים" (quality raw materials), handmade.
2. **Durability** — "עמידים במים, ים וזיעה" (waterproof, ocean & sweat resistant).
3. **Self-expression** — jewelry as personal statement, not decoration.
4. **Trust** — "אחריות מלאה לשנתיים" (full 2-year warranty), 4.9/5 rating.
5. **Gifting** — "אריזת מתנה בכל רכישה" (gift packaging with every purchase).

---

## 4. Tone of Voice

**Personality:** Confident, grounded, masculine. Aspirational but never arrogant.

**Do:**
- Speak direct, short sentences. Strength in restraint.
- Lead with meaning and identity ("more than jewelry").
- Reassure on durability and warranty — concrete, not hype.
- Use Hebrew as primary; English for punchy accent lines.
- Premium feel through simplicity, white space, confidence.

**Don't:**
- Over-explain or over-sell. No desperate discount-shouting.
- Cutesy, soft, or feminine framing.
- Cluttered copy or too many exclamation marks.
- Generic luxury clichés ("timeless elegance" filler).

**Voice in one line:** *Quiet confidence. Says less, means more.*
